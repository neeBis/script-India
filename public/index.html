OUR STORY

We are a non-profit organization ,who aspires to establish a platform for each and every tech enthusiast who desires to learn new technologies, meet new people and grow. We are big family of tech-lovers, from beginners to experts we have all of them at same plattform.

We are motivated from a single vision, that is to introduce more and more people to programming and most importantly bring more people towards development of open source softwares.
